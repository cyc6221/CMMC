Square-Root Barrier

The “square-root barrier” is the open problem of obtaining tight (no √-loss) security reductions for Fiat–Shamir signatures (e.g., Schnorr/GQ).
Standard rewinding/forking proofs often lose a √ factor because extraction needs two accepting transcripts.
